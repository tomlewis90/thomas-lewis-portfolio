About

Hello my name is Tom Lewis and in July 2024 I made the decision to commit to learning Software Engineering with a view of being in a position to make a career change and become a full time Software Engineer in 2025.

This was not an easy decision, I have a successful career, I work for a brilliant company and I am rewarded for it. I could have taken the "easy" road, stayed put, kept working hard and I have no doubt I would have had a great career. But I made the decision that it was time to follow something I was genuinely passionate and excited about. It had always been a seed growing in the back of my mind, but now was the time to let it flourish and see what it could grow into.

So here I am, a Construction and Development Specialist turned aspiring Software Engineer. In a constant search more knowledge and more practice. I have leared so much, but I know I have so much more to learn. I cannot wait to see where this path takes me, I truly believe it will be a success.

I am always interested to learn from other and would appriciate any and all guidance, experience and lessons learned anyone is will to share. So please contact me here, I would love to hear from you.
